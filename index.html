About me

Hi, welcome to my portfolio. I'm Victor Garcia, a computer engineering student at UCSB with a passion for bridging the gap between art and technology. Throughout the years, I've developed a wide range of projects, each one driven by a deep desire to explore the creative possibilities at the intersection of art and computer science. My journey started with creating animations, paintings, and digital sculptures. As I grew more technically advanced, so did my projects and goals, and I started creating art with technology.

I'm particularly interested in computer graphics, and I've used a variety of software tools to bring my artistic vision to life. I'm always seeking new ways to push the boundaries of what's possible in the field of computer graphics. I'm also interested in robotics and have designed, built, and programmed a projects that required a deep understanding of both hardware and software. My passion for art and technology has driven me to continuously learn new skills and explore new creative avenues. I'm always seeking to learn and grow.

If you have an artistic or technical project you'd like to bring to life, feel free to reach out to me. I'm always eager to collaborate and explore new creative possibilities.
